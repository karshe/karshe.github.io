Project Title

WordPress based website for college magazine.

Technology used

Backend PHP

Frontend HTML5

Database MySQL

CMS WordPress

JS framework jQuery

Cloud GAS

Objective

—
College magazine was concerned about online presence and editor wants common sharable dashboard for publishing. Our first choice was to go with CMS—and we chose WordPress as our management system. After installing, editor wants a typical magazine website so then our primary goal was to develop custom WordPress theme with mobile responsiveness.

How we do it

Phase I
INITIAL HOMEWORK & BRAINSTORMING

As we were first exposed to this project we made the mind that we will go with content management system—WordPress was first choice for us to go. Hence, we need to do some homework on how. WordPress driven website is presented in front of its user. This sommewhat gave us initial platform and same we presented in front of editor, we need to know first what our editor wants at first place. Our job was to give digital version to our editor's thought.

Phase II
Content Creation

To promote online readership we need to channelize our thought properly. Our job was to provide content creation guidelines to magazine team as their content was the soul for our WordPress driven website—these guidelines helped us to plan site accordingly. Content guidelines were heavily focused on engineers and youth—we included sideshows, quizzes, photo of day content.

Phase III
UI and UX

Now real job—we prepared initial drafts based on our mockups and editor's views. Our focus was heavily on content and hence mobile first, easy navigation, engaging contents and social interaction were initial priorities for us. We customized the magazine theme to suit our requirements—we later removed few elements in order to give proper experience to our readers.

Phase IV
Deployment & First Run

We deployed website at domain aspiremagaz.in and given access to magazine team—we later distributed experience sheet questionnaire in order to validate how much more work we need to do. Later, we deployed the edited version of website and we were ready with analytics setup.

Phase V
Analytics & Tweaks

After successful deployment, our task was to provide analysis results via automated mail and to tweak website layout accordingly. We also patched our server in order to deal with DDoS attacks.
