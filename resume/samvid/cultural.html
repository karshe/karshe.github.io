Talent Hunt  

General Rules

These rules and regulations shall be binding on each individual or group, who participates in registration process, for auditing in the program titled “SHOW ME WHAT YOU GOT” which is a part of 'SAMVID 2015'.
1. The program is a talent hunt competition where various people showcase their talents individually or in groups. Each participant competes against other participant and such the qualifying participant(s) shall proceed to next level of competition of the program, as per the format of the program.

2. The competition will consist of 3 rounds:
1st round : Auditions
2nd round : Eliminations
3rd round : Grand finale

Terms and Conditions

1. All the school and college students are allowed for the particular event. Individual should bring their identity card to prove that they are students.

2.Auditions judgement will be based on the performance of individual. Judges decision will be final and should be accepted by everyone.

3. In audition round, each participant will get 5 min. to present themselves and will not be extended under any circumstances.

4. Participant should bring props and other goods required during performance.

5. Elimination round will be on next day of audition. So individual should be prepared for that.

Fees

Single Participant INR 150

INR 50 extra for each participant, if it is a group performance.
